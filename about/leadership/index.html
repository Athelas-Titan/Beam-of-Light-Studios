Jack Henry Calverley

Director, Editor, Producer, Co-founder of Beam of Light Studios

Read Bio

I’m Jack Henry Calverley. I’m an artist from Riverdale, MI. I’ve written, directed edited and produced a handful of feature length and short films including MOUNT PLEASANT, Eye of The Beholder, SUNGLASSES I & II and Worst Day of My Life in Detroit. Musically, I was the lead member of the noise rock band TV VIOLENCE and am currently the vocalist and bassist of the rock band GUITAR. I was the founder and main creator of the creative productions studio known as ALRIGHT/BRAINLESS PRODUCTIONS (2018-2022). I am the co-founder of BEAM OF LIGHT STUDIOS and am currently in production of my latest work PINK PONIES.
